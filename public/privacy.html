Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Apex Coach is built with your privacy in mind. We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's core features. We do not sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Apex Coach, we collect the following information:

  • Account data — your email address, used for authentication via Supabase.
  • Training data — workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weight, RPE values, and dates you log within the app.
  • Workout plans — any session blueprints or templates you create.
  • App usage data — basic technical information needed for authentication and cloud sync (e.g. session tokens).

We do not collect location data, contacts, health data from Apple HealthKit, or any information beyond what is described above.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your training data across devices.
  • To power the progressive overload engine and personal records tracking.
  • To enable the Apex AI Coach feature (see Section 4).
  • To authenticate you and maintain your session securely.

3. Data Storage

Apex Coach uses a local-first architecture. Your data is stored in a SQLite database on your device and syncs to a cloud database (PostgreSQL via Supabase) when an internet connection is available.

Cloud data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. You can learn about Supabase's data practices at supabase.com/privacy.

4. AI Coach & Third-Party AI Services

The Apex AI Coach feature is powered by Google Gemini. When you send a message to the AI coach, your training history (workouts, sets, RPE values) and your message are sent to the Gemini API to generate a response.

This data is processed by Google in accordance with their API terms of service and privacy policy. You can review Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

If you prefer not to share your training data with the AI service, you can choose not to use the AI Coach feature. All other app functionality remains fully available without it.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with:

  • Supabase — for authentication and cloud database hosting.
  • Google (Gemini API) — only when you actively use the AI Coach feature.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. Upon request, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

7. Security

All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Authentication is handled via Supabase Auth using secure JWT tokens. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

8. Children's Privacy

Apex Coach is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact
